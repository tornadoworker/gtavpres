Slide 05 — Conclusion

  • Incredible open world with huge freedom

  • GTA Online keeps the game fresh with updates
  • Best-selling game of all time — proven quality

🎤 What to say — Slide 1 of 6

Introduction: "Hello everyone, today I'm going to talk about one of the most famous video games ever made — Grand Theft Auto 5, also known as GTA 5."

Follow-up: "My presentation will cover what the game is, its story, gameplay, and why it became such a huge cultural phenomenon. I'll also share my personal opinion at the end."

💡 Tip: Smile, make eye contact, and speak slowly. Don't rush — you have 5 minutes!

Opening: "Let me start with the basics. GTA 5 was released in September 2013 by a company called Rockstar Games."

Continue: "It's an open-world action-adventure game — which means you can explore a huge city freely. The game is set in Los Santos, a fictional city based on Los Angeles."

Key fact: "What makes GTA 5 special is its enormous budget — about 265 million dollars — making it one of the most expensive games ever produced."

💡 Tip: Stress "$265 million" — it surprises people and makes a great impression.

Opening: "Now let's talk about the story. GTA 5 has a very unique feature — you play as three different characters and can switch between them at any time."

Characters: "First, there's Michael, a retired criminal in the suburbs. Then Trevor — a violent, unpredictable man in the desert. And finally Franklin, a young guy trying to escape poverty."

Connection: "All three work together on big heists. The story is about crime, loyalty, and betrayal — very much like a Hollywood movie."

💡 Tip: "Heist" means a big robbery — mentioning this shows you know the vocabulary.

Opening: "So what can you actually do in GTA 5? The answer is — almost everything."

Activities: "You can drive cars, fly planes, ride motorcycles, go scuba diving, or even play golf and tennis. The map is larger than the previous three GTA games combined."

Online: "GTA Online is the multiplayer mode where up to 30 players share the same world. It's still incredibly popular today, more than 10 years after release."

💡 Tip: "Up to 30 players" sounds very natural in English — use it exactly like that.

Opening: "GTA 5 didn't just sell well — it changed the entire gaming industry."

Sales: "The game sold over 190 million copies worldwide. On its very first day, it made 800 million dollars — a world record at the time."

Controversy: "However, the game is also controversial. It contains a lot of violence and adult content, which led to political debates in many countries."

💡 Tip: "Controversial" is a great B2-level word. Pronounce it: con-TRO-ver-see-al.

Opening: "To conclude, I want to share my personal opinion on GTA 5."

Positives: "I think GTA 5 is genuinely impressive. The open world gives incredible freedom, and the three-character system makes the story much more interesting than a typical game."

Final verdict: "Overall, I believe GTA 5 is a masterpiece of game design — one of the most important games ever created. If you're old enough, it's absolutely worth playing."

Closing: "Thank you for listening. Does anyone have any questions?"

💡 Tip: End with a confident smile and "Any questions?" — it shows you're comfortable with the topic.
